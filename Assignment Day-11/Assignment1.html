Welcome to Andhra Pradesh

Explore the beauty, culture, and heritage of Andhra Pradesh!

Tirupati

Tirupati is home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world. The temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, is located on the seventh peak of the Tirumala Hills and attracts millions of devotees each year.

Araku Valley

Araku Valley is a stunning hill station in the Eastern Ghats, known for its coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture. It offers a perfect escape into nature, with beautiful landscapes and pleasant weather.

Vijayawada

Vijayawada, situated on the banks of the Krishna River, is a major city with significant historical and cultural importance. The Kanaka Durga Temple, Prakasam Barrage, and Undavalli Caves are among its popular attractions.
